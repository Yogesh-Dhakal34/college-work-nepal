BLEACH is a Japanese anime series based on  Tite Kubo's  ©manga© of the same name.

The story follows the adventures of Ichigo Kurosaki after he obtains the powers of a Soul Reaper—a death personification similar to the Grim Reaper—from another Soul Reaper, Rukia Kuchiki. His new abilities force him to take on the duties of defending humans from evil spirits and guiding departed souls to the afterlife.
